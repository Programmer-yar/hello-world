CHICKEN TIKKA RECIPE

Description

This is a famous barbeque category food. This food is normally enjoyed at dinner.It is cooked specially over coal stove. It is served and sold in units of siekh. Each siekh contains 4 chicken pieces. The good thing about this dish is that it is almost OilFree, has a balanced spice combination, light and cheap.

How to Prepare?

Method for making this dish is provided below. Make sure to collect the required ingrediants in correct amount before starting. After that please follow the step by step procedure for marination and cooking.

Ingrediants required

  • Chicken 2 kg
  • Ginger 1 teaspoon
  • Garlic 1 teaspoon
  • Lemon juice 8 teaspoons
  • Cooking Oil
  • Food Color
  • Tikka spice of any good company

Step by Step Procedure

  1. Cut Chicken pieces
  2. Add Ginger
  3. Add Garlic
  4. Add Tikka Masala
  5. Add food Color
  6. Leave it for 3 hours
  7. Cook it either in grill, oven or pan
