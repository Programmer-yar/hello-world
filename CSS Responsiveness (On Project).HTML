My Favourite Book

The Magic of thinking Big

By Dr. David Schwartz

A wonderful self help book which comprises the precious life lessons of Dr. David Schwartz. In this book, the author shows how our thinking effects our life. Anyone can achieve their dreams and life goals by taking control of their thoughts.

This Book contains a Total of 13 Chapters. In the begining of the book, the author talks about the importance of thinking big. Logically proving the point that most people struggling through tough financial conditions have small life goals or none at all.Just by raising the standards of our thinking we can accomplish way more than we think we can. Once we believe that we can do something, the how to do it develops on its own.

In the next chapters he talks about other components, which must be combined with big thinking to achieve anything a person wants. Those include thinking positive even in worst conditions, being honest, Taking action and adotpting leadership and decision making skills in order to enhance the effect of successful personality.

The 13 Chapters of this book are listed below:

  1. Believe You can Succeed and you will
  2. Cure yourself of exquistis, The failure disease
  3. Build Confidence and destroy fear
  4. How to think big
  5. How to think and dream creatively
  6. You are what you think you are
  7. Manage you environment Go first class
  8. Make your atitude your allies
  9. Think Right towards people
  10. Get the action habbit
  11. How to turn defeat into victory
  12. Use Goals to help you grow
  13. How to think like a leader
